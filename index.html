About Me

Hello! I'm Sukarn Srivastava, a Computer Science graduate with a passion for web development and a relentless desire to learn and create. My journey started with a fascination for turning ideas into digital realities, and I've been dedicated to honing my skills ever since.

Throughout my academic journey, I delved deep into the world of web development, gaining hands-on experience through diverse internships and trainings. From full-stack development to specializing in front-end technologies, I've cultivated a robust foundation in crafting user-friendly and visually appealing digital experiences.

My journey took an exciting turn when I ventured into freelancing, where I dived headfirst into front-end development. Over the course of a year, I collaborated with clients to bring their visions to life, creating responsive and dynamic user interfaces that seamlessly blend form and function.
My toolkit includes a range of technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js, Node.js, and more. From designing databases to building Python-based Flask web applications, I'm comfortable working across the spectrum of development. I thrive in IDEs like Visual Studio, Atom, and Eclipse, always adapting to the best tools for the job.

My ultimate goal is to continue evolving as a developer, staying attuned to emerging trends, and contributing my skills to innovative projects. I'm excited to embark on new challenges, collaborate with like-minded professionals, and turn imaginative ideas into tangible digital experiences.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Feel free to explore my work and journey further into my world of web development!
